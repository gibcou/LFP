“The Caller Returns”

Genre: Horror, Mystery, Thriller
Starring: Camille Winbush, Uche Jombo, Ayoola Ayolola, Kazuma Suzuki, Angela Katerine, Rick Gifford, Kenneth Okolie, Andrew Stewart-Jones
Director: Samson Alli
Producers:  Julie Campbell, Jimmy Mathew, Rick Gifford
Production Companies: Box Office Studios, Longhorn Film Productions
Status: Seeking Worldwide Distribution

Logline:
Yetunde learns that the mysterious calls from a prank caller are from a psychopath who murdered her parents years ago.  Anu will stop at nothing to fulfill his twisted vengeance. But Yetunde and her best friend, Farida aren’t going down a fight, and Anu is not the only one seeking revenge.

The Caller Returns is a story about failed fathers that create unintended consequences that impact their children.  Our story is about flawed, and real characters set in Nigeria and present-day Montana.  We pose the question for our audience, “whose revenge is it anyway?”
